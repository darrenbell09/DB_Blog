# Custom Skills

## `/new-post`

Write a new blog post in Darren's established voice and style.

**How it works:**
1. Asks 7 interview questions to extract your real experience on a topic
2. Confirms the answers with you before writing
3. Drafts a complete post with front matter, ready to publish
4. Offers to save and commit the file

**When to use:**
When you have a topic or an idea you want to turn into a blog post. The skill will ask about your actual experience and examples — you provide the expertise, it structures and writes.

**Invoke:**
```
/new-post
```
